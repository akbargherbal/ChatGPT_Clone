Can you explain what a static website is?

U
AI

Of course! A static website is delivered to a user's web browser exactly as it's stored. It consists of pre-rendered files (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) where the content is fixed and doesn't change based on user interaction or other data.

Key characteristics include:

  • Performance: They are generally very fast because they are just simple files served from a Content Delivery Network (CDN).
  • Security: With no database or complex server-side processing, there are fewer attack vectors, making them highly secure.
  • Simplicity & Cost: They are often easier and cheaper to develop and host.
